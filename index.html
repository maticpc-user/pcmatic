Frequently asked questions (PC Matic official)

🛡️ Why is PC Matic blocking my trusted application?

PC Matic uses a default-deny whitelist approach. You can add any blocked app via your online dashboard under 'Whitelist Manager'. Our support can assist instantly via live chat.

⏱️ How do I run a full system scan?

Open PC Matic application → Click "Scan" → Choose "Full System Scan". For scheduling, navigate to settings → Automate. Speed tips are on our guides section.

💳 How to cancel my PC Matic subscription?

Login to your PC Matic account → Billing → Cancel Subscription. No hidden fees. You can also request cancellation via support email. 30-day refund available.

🖥️ Does PC Matic work with Windows 11?

Yes, PC Matic is fully compatible with Windows 11/10, macOS, and Android. Our latest version includes kernel protections optimized for latest OS updates.
