Frequently asked questions (official answers)

📺 Why does Sling TV keep buffering?

Buffering can happen if your internet speed dips below 5 Mbps for HD. Try restarting your modem, lowering video quality, or connecting via Ethernet. Our app includes a "Network Test" under settings — we'll guide you step by step.

💰 How do I cancel my Sling TV subscription?

You can cancel anytime online: visit Sling.com → Account → Subscription → Cancel Plan. No hidden fees, and you keep access until the end of your billing cycle. For one-click assistance, use our live support chat.

🔄 Which devices are compatible with Sling TV?

Sling TV works on Roku, Amazon Fire TV, Apple TV, Chromecast, Android TV, iPhones, iPads, Samsung & LG Smart TVs, Xbox, and web browsers. Each device has a dedicated setup guide.

🌍 Can I watch local channels on Sling?

Local availability depends on your region. Sling Blue includes FOX and NBC in select markets, and Sling Orange focuses on ESPN/Disney. Use our "Channel Lookup" to see what’s live in your zip code.

🔒 I forgot my Sling password — how to reset?

Click "Forgot Password?" on the login screen. We'll send a secure reset link to your registered email. Still stuck? Our support team at tek.chat can assist you with verification.
